2. General notes on data processing

This website deliberately refrains from any form of tracking, analytics, advertising cookies or integration of external services. No personal data is transmitted to third parties when accessing this website.

3. Server log files

When this website is accessed, the hosting provider collects technically necessary data in server log files which your browser transmits automatically: IP address, date and time of the request, page accessed, referrer URL, browser used and operating system.

The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in the technically error-free operation of the website). This data is not merged with other data sources and is deleted after a short period.

GitHub processes technically necessary data (in particular IP addresses) in the course of delivering the website. The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR. For data transfers to the USA, GitHub relies on the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

5. Fonts

This website uses only locally hosted fonts. No fonts from external providers (e.g. Google Fonts) are loaded. Therefore, no IP address is transmitted to third parties.

If you contact us by e-mail, your information will be stored for the purpose of processing the request and for any follow-up questions. The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) lit. b or f GDPR. No data is passed on to third parties.
